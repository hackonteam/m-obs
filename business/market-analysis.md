# Market Analysis

## Executive Summary

M-OBS targets the rapidly growing blockchain infrastructure monitoring market, specifically focusing on Layer 2 observability. Our initial beachhead is the Mantle ecosystem ($50M+ TVL, 100+ dApps), with expansion planned to other L2s (Arbitrum, Optimism, Base) representing a combined $10B+ TVL market.

**Key Findings:**
- **TAM:** $500M+ (blockchain infrastructure monitoring)
- **SAM:** $50M (L2 observability tools)
- **SOM:** $5M (Mantle + 2-3 major L2s, Year 3)
- **Growth Rate:** 150%+ YoY (blockchain adoption)
- **Market Gap:** No Mantle-specific monitoring tools exist

## Risk Analysis

### Market Risks

**1. Bear Market Impact**
- **Risk:** Crypto crash reduces budgets
- **Likelihood:** Medium (cyclical)
- **Mitigation:** Flexible pricing, focus on ROI, cost-saving features

**2. Consolidation**
- **Risk:** Big player (Alchemy, Tenderly) acquires competitors
- **Likelihood:** Medium
- **Mitigation:** Open source, differentiated features, community

**3. Mantle Ecosystem Stagnation**
- **Risk:** Mantle doesn't grow as expected
- **Likelihood:** Low
- **Mitigation:** Multi-chain strategy from Year 1

---

**1. Tenderly Lowers Prices**
