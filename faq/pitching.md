**Response:**
> "Tenderly is amazing for simulation and debugging, but it's expensive ($500-2,000/month) and generic across all chains. We're laser-focused on Mantle, which means:

**Response:**
> "Absolutely, and many teams try. But here's what we learned building M-OBS:
> - It took 6 hours of focused development, but weeks of production hardening
> - Handling blockchain reorgs is tricky
> - RPC provider failover logic is complex
> - You need 24/7 uptime infrastructure
>
> At $49-199/month, we're cheaper than half a developer's day. Why spend weeks building and maintaining this when you could be building your core product?
